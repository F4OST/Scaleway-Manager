About
Who created this app ?

I am only one developer who basically wanted to create a Scaleway application for personal use and then I realized that it could be useful for many other people. I'm a student in France in an IT school. You can follow me on Twitter: @matth_prost


Is it an official application published by Scaleway ?

Absolutely not ! Before creating the app I asked Scaleway if I could publish it and they give me their agreement. So here we are !


Why did you created this application ?

As Scaleway is based on time = money, I wanted an easier way to start/stop my servers to save money and I think an application is the solution. You can start and stop it in just one click.


Why is this application free and without ads ?

I'm not a big fan of advertising, I think it destroy the design of an application. Maybe later I will add advertising.


I have other questions, where can I ask ?

At the home page you have an icon of a life buoy on the bottom where you can find my website, a way to contact me or to report a bug. You can also contact me on Twitter: @matth_prost. Don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions!


What about our data/privacy ?

I do not sell or personally use your data at any moment. I just use Scaleway API to allow you to connect, get your servers, start them, ect. Also this project is Open Source and available on GitHub. If you're still worried about your account I recommend you to activate Two-Factor Authentication
