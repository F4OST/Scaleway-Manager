About
Who created this app ?

I am only one developer who basically wanted to create a Scaleway application for personal use and then realized that it could be useful for many other people. I'm a student in France in an IT school.


Is this an official application published by Scaleway ?

Absolutely not ! Before creating the app I asked Scaleway if I could publish it and they give me their agreement. So here we are !


Why did you created this application ?

As Scaleway is based on time = money, I wanted an easier way to start/stop my servers to save money and I think an application is the solution. You can start and stop it in just one click.


Why is this application free and without ads ?

I'm not a big fan of advertising, I think it destroy the design of an application. You can still support me by making a donation trough the donation system, the money will be use for paying Apple developer account.


I have other questions, where can I ask ?

At the home page you have an icon of a life buoy on the bottom where you can find my website, a way to contact me or to report a bug. Don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions!


What about our data/privacy ?

I do not sell or personally use your data at any moment. I just use Scaleway API to allow you to connect, get your servers, start them, ect. Also this project is Open Source, it means that everybody can see the code so you can see how I use your data. But if you're still worried about your account I recommend you to activate Two-Factor Authentication
